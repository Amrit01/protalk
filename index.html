ProTalk on GitHub

Welcome to the ProTalk project on GitHub! We are a highly active open source project with a growing community of developers. We'd very much like YOU to join us and help make ProTalk the #1 online resource for programming knowledge in audio/video format.

Everyone is welcome

We invite contributions from developers of all skill levels, from seasoned professionals and open-source stalwarts to first-time open source contributors or novices. The development team is ready to provide support and answer questions on the ProTalk IRC channel on freenode.

Join Us

Contributing to an open source project is an excellent way to learn new skills and get involved in the developer community (it doesn't do your CV or resume any harm either!). You can spend as little or as much time on it as you like, all we ask is that you stick with us and see your work through to completion.

We'll show you how

If you're thinking of taking the plunge into open source, come and join the ProTalk team and we'll show you the ropes.

Step by step instructions on how to install the project and get started contributing are set out in the ProTalk Wiki. We have plenty of open issues for you to sharpen your skills on, ranging from easy right through to quite challenging, and we have lots of ideas for new features under discussion on IRC.
